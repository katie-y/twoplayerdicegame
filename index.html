Rules


Take turns to roll the die as many times as you like.
You can choose to hold your current score and add it to your total score. It will then be the other player's turn.
If you roll a 1 your current score will reset to 0 and it's your opponent's turn.
The first person to get their total score over 20 is the winner!

Have fun!
