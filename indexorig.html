Rules


Take turns to roll the die as many times as you like.
If you roll a 1 your current score will reset to zero and it's the next player's turn.
You can hold your turn at anytime, this will add the score of your current turn to your total score. It will then be the other player's turn.
The first person to get their total score over 20 is the winner!

Have fun!
